Pick-A-Place

This is a small web application designed to help you choose a place to eat. In the future it will do the following:

  • All you to specify a type of restaurant, and show you your choices.
  • Allow you to specify any place random, and will display the web page of a random restaurant.
  • Allow you to add a new restaurant to the application, as well as its URL.
